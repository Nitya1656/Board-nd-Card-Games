Board & Card Games

Chess

Strategy board game. Setup: 8x8 grid, each player gets 16 pieces. 2 players. Objective: checkmate the king.

Uno

Fast-paced card game. Setup: Deal 7 cards. Match color/number. 2–10 players. First to run out wins.

Codenames

Team word game. Setup: 25-word grid. Guess based on clues. 4–8 players. Win by finding your team's words first.

Scrabble

Word-building board game. Setup: Tile rack + board. 2–4 players. Score with long, high-value words.

Monopoly

Classic property trading game. Setup: Banker, pieces, cards. 2–6 players. Bankrupt everyone else to win.

Exploding Kittens

Fast-paced card chaos. Setup: Remove Exploding cards. Avoid them. 2–5 players. Last player standing wins.
